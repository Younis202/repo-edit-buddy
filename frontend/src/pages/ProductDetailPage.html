Just the FAQs

Got a less-frequently asked question? Email us at hello@livelarq.com to ask it.

The LARQ Pitcher features our Nano Zero filter which removes lead, chlorine, PFAS (PFOA/PFOS), and more.

The LARQ Pitcher does not include our PureVis™ technology.

The LARQ Pitcher includes 1 Advanced Filter.
Depending on your household and local water quality, the LARQ Filter should last up to 2 months of use or filters up to 60 gallons (227 liters) of water.

LARQ Bottle Swig Top is for use with cold or room temperature water only. It can keep water cold for up to 24 hours. We’d suggest the LARQ Bottle Twist Top for both hot or cold features.

You can put the LARQ Bottle in the dishwasher (the cap should go on the top rack). However, we recommend hand washing it with warm water and soap for optimal product care.
